Brand Voice · master-brand-document.md

Confident

We know our craft. Authority without arrogance. Direct language that demonstrates expertise.

Warm

Approachable and human. Never cold or corporate. Every interaction should feel like a conversation.

Expert

We guide, educate, and simplify. Show the thinking behind the recommendation.

Sincere

Every promise is real. No price on site — only conversations. Gifting is personal, not transactional.

Key brand rule: No prices displayed on the website. All pricing happens through personal consultation (quote form or WhatsApp). This positions every gift as a bespoke conversation, not a catalogue transaction.
