A corporate gift should feel like a seamless extension of your brand. We offer refined, understated customisation options that elevate the unboxing experience without making it feel like a billboard.

01. Logo Branding

Precision identity integration.

We apply your corporate identity to physical products using high-end techniques like laser engraving, subtle screen printing, and debossing. The goal is a premium, retail-quality finish that employees and clients are proud to carry into a boardroom.

02. The Unboxing Experience

Bespoke packaging & colorways.

The unboxing moment sets the tone. We offer custom-printed outer sleeves, rigid box branding, and hand-tied ribbons color-coordinated to match your exact brand guidelines. It ensures your corporate identity is felt before the box is even opened.

03. The Human Touch

Personalized notes & inserts.

Gifting is about connection. We design and print high-GSM custom greeting cards, founder letters, and specialized insert guides (like brewing instructions or welcome messages) that fit perfectly within the internal architecture of your chosen hamper.
