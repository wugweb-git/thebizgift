Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At The Biz Gift, we take your corporate data and personal information seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our B2B showroom and quoting services.

1. Information We Collect

When you request a quote or contact us, we collect standard business information including your name, corporate email address, phone number, and company details. This data is securely routed to our internal CRM (Airtable) exclusively for the purpose of fulfilling your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to provide tailored corporate gifting proposals, process orders, and communicate regarding your specific project requirements. We do not sell your data to third-party marketing agencies.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure that your business details and corporate identity assets (such as logos provided for customisation) remain confidential and protected.
